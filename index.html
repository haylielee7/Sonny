Son Heung-min

Professional Footballer

Personal Biography

Son Heung-min is a South Korean professional footballer who plays as a forward for Tottenham Hotspur and the South Korean national team. He captains both teams and is often regarded as the best Asian footballer of all time. He is often credited for his speed, his ability to play and score with both feet, and his finishing. Son is often referred to as "Sonny."

He was born on July 8, 1992, in Chuncheon, Gangwon, South Korea (he's Haylie's birthday twin!). He is fluent in English, Korean, and German. Son resides in both Tottenham, England, and Seoul, South Korea, splitting his time between both countries.

Career

Prior to joining Tottenham Hotspur, Son began his football journey at the FC Seoul Academy, where he went from ball boy to youth player. He then joined Hamburger SV, followed by a transfer to Bundesliga, where he signed for Bayer Leverkusen. Following this signing, Son eventually signed with Premier League club Tottenham Hotspur for around $28 million, making him the most expensive Asian player in football history.

Since signing for Tottenham Hotspur, Son has become a crucial member to the team, starting for nearly every match. He has brought thousands of fans to Tottenham Hotspur stadium and scored hundreds of goals.
